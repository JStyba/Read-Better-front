ABOUT

READ BETTER is a project aiming to enhance learning experience. Nowadays, the internet has become a major tool when it comes to language learning, and yet it is not used in its full potential. Instead of allowing people to interact with the real language, most online tools focus on mimicking it. I believe people should have the opportunity to interact with the target language on their own terms, and that is what Read Better is striving to achieve.

How it works:
The main idea is that you can display a web page inside of Read-Better, and all the words on that web page become interactive
in a sense that you can either get their English dictionary definitions (from Oxford Dictionary API) or Polish direct translation (from Glosbe API).
The words that you decide to save are stored in My Read Better and can be retrieved at will at managed. I'm working tirelessly on Read-Better to make it ... well better. The next step is to create a space to help you memorize your saved words.

  • Read-Better is designed for English web pages and PDF files.
  • Read-Better was created to work under Google Chrome.
  • The look of displayed web pages might sometimes look worse than the original. Documents though should look fine
